President's Voice

It‟s my pleasure to share few words on my Presidentship of this Rotary year 2020-21. First of all I would like to thank Rotaract movement for giving me such a wonderful opportunity in life. It has been such an inspiration for me to be as president for this prestigious club. All the activities done in the past months is to fulfill the need of our club members and the society. The service that we do through Rotaract is such an opportunity to exhibit my skills along with a group of many other people with high creativeness and passion towards the movement. I‟ll maintain the decorum and heart of the club to stay active in the entire year and make TEXCITY legacy in the heights.

Secretary's Voice

It gives me immense pleasure to share my experience of this responsibility. Secretaryship is one of the huge tasks to make it success. It plays the vital role in the club. I feel that im maintaining it in the positive way in this Rotary year till now. This role taught me lot of things in the last months and loads of learning. Maintaining such legacy after tremendous efforts by past secretaries is comparatively tough but this gives me huge experience to learn and evolve. I feel that it also matters in maintaining the clubs legacy throughout the year in the monthly reports as well. Rotaract is the one platform were we learn a lot, exhibit a lot and enjoy a lot.
