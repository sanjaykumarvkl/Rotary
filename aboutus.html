About Us

Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Texcity has a legacy from RI District 320 to 3201 was started in the year 2008 with the motto of “self-development and fellowship through service” still gleams with the same and equal pride. The club was chartered on 13th February 2008, sponsored by Rotary Club of Coimbatore Texcity. Adding a feather to its crown, PDRR.Rtr.CVR.karthikeyan from Rotaract club of Coimbatore Texcity served as the Second DRR of RI District 3201. Currently it is under the leadership of Rtr.Logesh Bharani.B as the President. It belongs to Rotary International District 3201, Zone 5 in South Asia and Group 3 in the District.

The club ventures scope, liberty and new trail to the young Rotaractors and cultivates devotion, passion and inner strength. It makes difference by being unique with motivation, discipline, pure heart, excellence and leadership. It substantiates with the sincerity of purpose of its members. This gleam of Rotaract Club of Coimbatore Texcity outlives eternally.

Rotary Rotaract Relations

Our Parent Rotary is Rotary Club of Coimbatore Texcity. Rotary Club of Coimbatore Texcity consists of a vibrant group of 62 Rotarians and their families. A healthy mix of people from different vocations, was chartered on 4th April, 1990 with Rtn Rajshree Pathy as Charter President. Currently it is under the leadership of Rtn MD Dr Velswamy Ravendran as the President (2020-2021). The Rotary club of Coimbatore Texcity has been in the limelight for excellent works in all avenues of service, especially Youth Service and Vocational Service. We wish to maintain the same healthy relationship with our parent rotary.
